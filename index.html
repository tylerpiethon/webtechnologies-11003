    • Progressive Enhacement

    Progressive enhancement makes sure the content of a site is accessible to all users regardless of what the person is using to access a site. Progressive enhancement is important in modern web development because of it prioritizing a website content and functionality for all users. Especially for users that have an older browser or device. According to the site https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Glossary/Progressive_Enhancement
